=== Microservices Interview Questions ===

Q1: What are microservices?
A1: Architectural style where application is composed of small, independent services.

Q2: What is API Gateway?
A2: Single entry point for all clients, handles routing, auth, rate limiting.

What are microservices?
Answer: Microservices are small, loosely coupled services that focus on a single business capability.
Each service has its own data store, logic, and deployment pipeline,
making it independently scalable and deployable.

MNC-level insight: Highlight that microservices enable agility, fault isolation, and technology diversity.
For example, one service can use Spring Boot with PostgreSQL, while another uses Node.js with MongoDB,
without impacting the overall system.

Difference between monolithic and microservices architecture
Answer: added by spring boot
Monolithic: Single codebase, tightly coupled, difficult to scale, one failure can affect the entire system.
Microservices: Modular, independently deployable, fault-tolerant, easier to scale specific services.
MNC-level insight: Stress that microservices align with DevOps and CI/CD pipelines,
enabling faster releases and cloud-native scalability.
